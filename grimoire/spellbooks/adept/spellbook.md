This spellbook is active in every run.

It holds the universal control surface that lets a spirit stay responsive while widening work deliberately.

Use it for:

- spellbook discovery and opening
- messaging lanes and thread-safe acknowledgements
- emanations and other bounded subruns
- emergency halt
- ritual charge reclamation

The emanation casts are especially important because they show how charge should work:

- the cast itself may cost `0`
- the emanation still allocates charge from the source run
- the source run keeps responsibility for orchestration, review, and integration
- stronger or more specialized emanations widen the work without pretending that widening is free

Each concrete cast in this spellbook lives in its own folder beside this manifest.
